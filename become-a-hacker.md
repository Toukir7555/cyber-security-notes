## 🚶 Walkthrough

### 🔹 What is a Hacker?
A hacker is an individual who uses technical knowledge to identify and exploit vulnerabilities in systems. Ethical hackers (white-hat) use these skills legally to improve security.

---

### 🔹 Types of Hackers
- **White Hat** – Ethical and legal security professionals  
- **Black Hat** – Malicious attackers  
- **Grey Hat** – Operate between legal and illegal boundaries  

---

### 🔹 Cybersecurity Roles
- **Red Team** – Focuses on attacking systems (Pentesting)  
- **Blue Team** – Focuses on defending systems (Monitoring & Response)  

---

### 🔹 Required Skills
To become a penetration tester, the following skills are essential:
- Networking (TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP)  
- Linux fundamentals  
- Web application basics  
- Analytical and problem-solving mindset
